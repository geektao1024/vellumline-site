Vellum & Line now has a browser-based studio path for creating printable custom map posters from cities, addresses, coordinates, and meaningful places.

This baseline focuses on the core workflow:

- Search for a real place or coordinate.
- Adjust the map style and poster composition.
- Add context such as route lines, markers, and poster text.
- Export artwork for printing or design handoff.

The next product notes will stay focused on map design, route support, export quality, and the SEO landing pages that guide visitors into the studio.
