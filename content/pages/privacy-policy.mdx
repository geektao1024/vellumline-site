## Introduction

Vellum & Line is an independent browser-based map poster maker for creating custom map art from places, routes, and map data. This privacy policy explains what information may be collected when you visit vellumline.com or use the Vellum & Line studio.

Vellum & Line is independent and is not affiliated with Terraink or TerraInk.

## Information We May Collect

We aim to keep data collection minimal and relevant to operating the site.

1. **Usage information**
   - Pages visited, referring URLs, device/browser details, and basic interaction events may be collected through analytics tools if enabled.
   - This helps us understand which Terraink, map poster, and product pages are useful.

2. **Studio activity**
   - Map poster settings may be processed in your browser so the studio can render and export artwork.
   - Route or GPX files, if used, are handled for the purpose of drawing a route poster.

3. **Contact information**
   - If you contact us by email, we may keep your email address and message so we can respond.

4. **Cookies and local storage**
   - The site may use cookies or local storage for preferences, analytics attribution, and basic product behavior.

## How We Use Information

We use information to operate the site, improve the Vellum & Line studio, understand search and conversion paths, respond to support requests, and protect the service from abuse.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help operate hosting, analytics, email, or security, or when required by law.

## Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no internet service can guarantee absolute security.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. The latest version will be posted on this page.
